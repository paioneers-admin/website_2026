Our Research Methodology

Signal-led market intelligence, not desk research

01

Source triangulation

KvK filings, LinkedIn hiring patterns, UWV labor data, export-license statistics, consortium rosters, earnings transcripts, and customer press releases. Multiple independent signals per thesis.

02

EDP identification

Existential Data Points are the metrics that flip from operational annoyance to balance-sheet event. We identify the thresholds where a data point becomes commercially actionable.

GTM operationalisation

Every finding maps to a detection method, an urgency logic, and a commercial entry point. Research without a GTM application stays on the shelf.
